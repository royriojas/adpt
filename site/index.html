About Kidapt

Kidapt, Inc. is an innovative media company dedicated to using post-PC device technology, smart storytelling, and research-based curriculum in partnership with Stanford University Researchers to create entertaining and fun content that helps children learn. Our character-based episodic vignettes will initially center around pre-school-aged children, engaging them with puzzles and games interwoven within an immersive story arc that drives kindergarten preparedness.

Subsequently our platform will expand to address prevailing issues facing the over 42 million "early learners" (PreK-6 ages 3-11) in the US. We believe that parents of these early learners, who often share smartphones, tablets and other connected devices with their children, are searching for a richer, more complete outlet that they can rely on to both entertain and educate.

About Leo's Pad

Our first offering, Leo's Pad, is based on Leonardo Da Vinci as an 8 year old and a cadre of kid characters, each a playfully re-imagined historical figure, including Gally (Galileo), Shakes (Shakespeare), Flo (Florence Nightingale), etc. Through Leo's Pad we seek to deliver an evergreen subscription service to children and parents, representing much more than the typical single purpose apps that currently exist.
